# Mano Chess

A web-based chess game with realistic coins, a large, responsive board, animated background, and both local and online multiplayer support.

## Features

- Realistic black and white chess pieces
- Large, mobile-friendly chess board
- Animated, colorful background
- Local two-player mode
- (Planned) Online multiplayer
- Game title: Mano Chess

## Setup

1. Clone/download the repo.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.
